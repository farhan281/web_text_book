Matan al-Manar: A Summary of Hanafi Usul
Describing the nature of Matan al-Manar and the author's death

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will be able to identify the sources and structure of Matan al-Manar and state the author's death year.

Core Idea
Manar is a concise summary of Bazdawi and Sarakhsi's usul works, with a reliance on Bazdawi's arrangement.

This passage introduces Matan al-Manar, a foundational text in Hanafi usul al-fiqh. It explains that the text is a summary of two major works: Usul al-Bazdawi by Fakhru al-Islam al-Bazdawi and Usul al-Sarakhsi by Shams al-Aimmah al-Sarakhsi. The author, often referred to as the 'matn', also wrote an extensive commentary on his own text called Kashf al-Asrar. The passage also provides the author's death details, noting that he passed away in 710 AH in Baghdad, though his birth year is unknown.

Guided Path
1
Identify the base works
Determine which two earlier usul works Matan al-Manar summarizes. The passage names Fakhru al-Islam al-Bazdawi and Shams al-Aimmah al-Sarakhsi.

Note the arrangement preference
Observe that the text follows the arrangement of Usul al-Bazdawi more closely than that of al-Sarakhsi.
This indicates the author's methodological reliance on al-Bazdawi.
3
Recognize the author's own commentary
The author wrote an expansive commentary on his own text named Kashf al-Asrar fi Sharh al-Manar, highlighting the text's importance and depth.
This commentary is itself a significant work in Hanafi usul.
4

Knowing the death year helps place the text in historical context.

Commentary
This passage introduces the text 'Matan al-Manar' as a summary of two foundational works in Hanafi usul: al-Bazdawi's 'Usul al-Bazdawi' and al-Sarakhsi's 'Usul al-Sarakhsi'. It notes that the organization primarily follows that of al-Bazdawi. The author, known as the 'matn' (text author), also wrote a commentary on his own work called 'Kashf al-Asrar', indicating the importance of the text. The death note confirms that al-Nasafi passed away in 710 AH in Baghdad, though his birth year is not recorded.

Key Terms
متن المنار (Matan al-Manar): A concise summary of Hanafi usul al-fiqh based on the works of al-Bazdawi and al-Sarakhsi.
فخر الاسلام البزدوي (Fakhru al-Islam al-Bazdawi): A major Hanafi usul scholar whose work Usul al-Bazdawi is one of the sources of Manar.
شمس الائمة السرخسي (Shams al-Aimmah al-Sarakhsi): A prominent Hanafi jurist and usuli, author of Usul al-Sarakhsi, another source of Manar.
كشف الاسرار في شرح المنار (Kashf al-Asrar fi Sharh al-Manar): The author's own commentary on Matan al-Manar, providing detailed proofs and explanations.

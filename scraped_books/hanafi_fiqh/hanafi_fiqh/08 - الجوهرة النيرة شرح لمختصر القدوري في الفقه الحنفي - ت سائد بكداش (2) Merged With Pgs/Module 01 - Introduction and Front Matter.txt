Introducing Al-Jawharah al-Nayrah and Its Authors
Title page of Al-Jawharah al-Nayrah.

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will be able to identify the title, author, base text, and scholarly significance of Al-Jawharah al-Nayrah.

Core Idea
Al-Jawharah al-Nayrah is a Hanafi commentary on al-Quduri by al-Haddad — a standard teaching text.

This title page introduces the second volume of a major Hanafi fiqh commentary. Understanding the book's identity and its relationship to the foundational matn (Mukhtasar al-Quduri) is essential for navigating the tradition.

Guided Path
1
Identify the book title
Read the Arabic title 'الجوهرة النيرة' (The Luminous Jewel) and note that it is a sharh (commentary).
The title indicates the book's purpose: to illuminate the concise text of al-Quduri.
2
Identify the commentary author
Find the name 'الإمام أبي بكر بن علي الحداد' (Imam Abu Bakr ibn Ali al-Haddad) and note his approximate death date (720/800 AH).
Al-Haddad is known for this widely-used commentary.
3
Identify the base text (matn) and its author
The phrase 'المختصر الإمام القُدُوري' refers to Mukhtasar al-Quduri by Imam Ahmad ibn Muhammad al-Quduri (362-428 AH), a foundational Hanafi primer.
Mukhtasar al-Quduri is one of the most important concise Hanafi texts.
4
Note editor and publisher
The editor is Prof. Dr. Sa'id Bakdash, and the publisher is 'أروقة' (Ariqah). Volume Two indicates this is a multi-volume work.
These details help locate the specific edition.
5
Understand the nature of a sharh
A sharh expands on the matn, providing proofs, explanations, and additional rulings from the Hanafi school.
Commentaries like this are central to Hanafi education.

Commentary
This is the title page of the second volume of Al-Jawharah al-Nayrah, a standard Hanafi fiqh commentary by Imam Abu Bakr ibn Ali al-Haddad (d. 800 AH or 720 AH – the date is unclear in the text). The work is a sharh (commentary) on Mukhtasar al-Quduri, a foundational matn (text) of the Hanafi school written by Imam Ahmad ibn Muhammad al-Quduri (d. 428 AH). The editor is Professor Dr. Sa'id Bakdash. The publisher is Ariqah (أروقة). This volume continues the commentary on the Quduri text, covering later chapters of Hanafi fiqh.

Key Terms
الجوهرة النيرة (al-Jawharah al-Nayrah): The title of the commentary: 'The Luminous Jewel'. It is a Hanafi fiqh commentary on Mukhtasar al-Quduri.
المختصر القدوري (Mukhtasar al-Quduri): A concise Hanafi fiqh text by Imam al-Quduri (d. 428 AH), widely used as a primer in the Hanafi school.
شرح (sharh): Commentary or exposition that explains and expands upon a base text (matn).

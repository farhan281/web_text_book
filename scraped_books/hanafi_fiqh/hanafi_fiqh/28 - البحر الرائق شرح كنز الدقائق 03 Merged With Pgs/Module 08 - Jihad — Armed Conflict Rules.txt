Permissibility of Intercourse with Spoils' Slave Girls: The Condition of Proper Distribution
Fatwa on permissibility of intercourse with slave girls from war spoils

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will be able to state the Hanafi condition for permissibility of intercourse with a slave girl from war booty and apply it to a given scenario.

Core Idea
Intercourse with a slave girl from spoils is only halal if the spoils were lawfully divided by the imam.

This lesson examines a fatwa from al-Bahr al-Ra'iq addressing a crucial question: when is it permissible to have intimate relations with a slave girl purchased from a fighter (ghazah)? The Hanafi position emphasizes that ownership must be established through a valid shari division of spoils. We will explore the ruling and its condition.

Guided Path
1
Identify the act and its default ruling
The act in question is intercourse (wat') with a slave girl. The default ruling is that it is permissible only if one has lawful ownership over her.
Ownership must be free from any doubt (shubha).
2
Identify the condition for validity
The key condition here is that the slave girl must have been acquired through a lawful division of war spoils by the imam (qisma shar'iyya). Without this, ownership is doubtful.
The fatwa mentions 'qisma shar'iyya' – proper Islamic distribution.
3
Understand the role of tanfil
Tanfil is when the imam distributes the entire spoils to the fighters after deducting khums. This removes the doubt about ownership.
Tanfīl kullī means total distribution, leaving no share for the public treasury.
4
Apply to the historical case
The fatwa cites the year 948 AH when a total tanfil occurred, so slave girls from that campaign are permissible.
For other times, the general rule applies: no lawful division means it is not permissible.

From the submissions of Abu al-Sa'ud: And is it permissible to have intercourse with slave girls bought from the ghazah (fighters) nowadays, where there is doubt about their division in the lawful manner? He answered: There is no lawful division in our time, but in the year 948 AH, total tanfil (distribution of spoils) occurred, so after giving the khums, no doubt remains. End.

Commentary
This is a fatwa question presented to the commentator. The question asks about a practical issue: whether it is permissible for a man to have intercourse with a slave girl who was bought from those who acquired her as part of war booty (ghazah), given the prevalent doubt about whether the distribution of spoils was done properly according to Islamic law. The answer states that in the current time (the writer's era), there is no proper shari division of spoils, so normally it would not be permissible. However, for the year 948 AH, a total tanfil (granting of spoils) had occurred, meaning the imam distributed the entire spoils (after deducting khums) to the fighters, and therefore there is no doubt about ownership in that case. The implication is that for other times, one should be cautious.

Key Terms
تنفيل كلي (tanfīl kullī): Total distribution of spoils by the imam to the fighters after taking the khums.
قسمة شرعية (qisma shar'iyya): Lawful division of war spoils according to Shari'ah, typically by the imam or his deputy.
شبهة (shubha): Doubt or ambiguity that affects the legal ruling.

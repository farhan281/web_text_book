Commentary

The fourth calls him the 'renewer (mujaddid) of the sixth century' of the Hijra. The fifth, from al-Suyuti's poetic work, places al-Razi as the sixth in his list of great scholars, comparing him to al-Shafi'i. The sixth, from the biographer al-Qifti or similar, extols his superiority in fiqh, usul, kalam, and philosophy, noting his refutation of Ibn Sina and the widespread use of his works.

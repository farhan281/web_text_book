Biographical Sources for the Author
Lists four major biographical sources used for the author's life

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will identify the four major biographical works used to document the author's life.

Core Idea
Four essential biographical works illuminate the author's life.

The author's biography is drawn from several classical sources. This lesson introduces four key works that scholars rely on for his life. Understanding these sources helps situate the author within the scholarly tradition.

Guided Path
1
Read the passage
Read the Arabic list and its translation. Notice the format: each source is given with its author and reference.
2
Identify the four works
List the four works: Shadharat al-Dhahab, al-Badr al-Tali', Hadiyat al-'Arifin, Kashf al-Zunun.
These are well-known biographical and bibliographical works.
3
Consider their purpose
Think about why the author cites these sources. They provide authoritative information about the subject's life and works.

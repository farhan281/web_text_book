Definition of Attributes of Meanings (Sifāt al-Maʿānī)
Sanusi defines attributes of meanings and explains their nature and relation to the essence.

Learning Objective

Core Idea
Attributes of meanings exist in themselves but subsist only in the essence, necessitating a judgment through correlation.

Sanusi now introduces the third category of divine attributes: attributes of meanings (sifāt al-maʿānī). He defines them as existential attributes that subsist in the essence and necessitate a judgment. He also clarifies two potential misunderstandings: that they could exist independently, and that 'necessitating' implies causation. This lesson unpacks these points so you can correctly understand this key classification.

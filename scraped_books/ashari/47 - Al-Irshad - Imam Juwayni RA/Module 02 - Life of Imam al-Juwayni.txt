Commentary
This passage concedes al-Ghazali's unique distinction. Despite not being chronologically first, he is recognized as the most prominent figure in this area. His two famous works on philosophy, Maqasid al-Falasifa (which presents philosophical doctrines) and Tahafut al-Falasifa (which refutes them), have had a lasting influence.

Ghazali's Fiqh Works
List of Ghazali's fiqh and legal works with brief descriptions.

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will be familiar with Imam Ghazali's major contributions to Shafi'i fiqh and the structure of his legal writings.

Core Idea
Ghazali's fiqh works were concise yet comprehensive, showing his masterful grasp of Shafi'i jurisprudence.

Imam Ghazali wrote extensively across many fields, but his legal works remain foundational for Shafi'i fiqh. This passage introduces his key fiqh texts, including the concise al-Wajiz and al-Khulasa, as well as his fatwa collections. The passage also highlights his careful methodology in organizing his books.

Guided Path
1
Read the Arabic passage carefully
Begin by reading the Arabic text once, noting the names of the books mentioned. Then read the English translation to ensure you grasp the meaning.
Notice that the passage uses the term 'ومن تصانيفه أيضا' to introduce the list.
2
Identify the fiqh works
The passage lists several works: al-Wajiz, al-Khulasa, Kitab al-Fatawa, and mentions less famous fatwas. Look for the descriptions of each work, such as 'mujallad duna al-Tanbih' (a volume smaller than al-Tanbih) for al-Khulasa.
Al-Wajiz and al-Khulasa are well-known summaries of Shafi'i fiqh.
3
Understand the significance of the list
Ask yourself why it is important to note that his fatwa collection includes 190 issues and is not arranged. This shows his output despite disorganization.
4
Connect to his methodology
The first part of the passage (not included in this lesson's source) mentions Ghazali's use of commentaries. Reflect on how his fiqh works were built on earlier scholarship.
This lesson focuses on his own works, but his methodology is part of his legacy.

Commentary
This passage is a bibliographical listing of Imam Ghazali's major works. It begins by mentioning that Ghazali adopted a refined arrangement in his books, and that his commentary on al-Muhadhdhab (by Abu Ishaq al-Shirazi) and his notes on Qadi Husayn's commentary were important sources. Then it lists his independent works: 'al-Wajiz' and 'al-Khulasa' are concise works in Shafi'i fiqh. 'Kitab al-Fatawa' contains 190 unorganized legal opinions, with another lesser-known set. 'Ihya' Ulum al-Din' is described as 'the wonder' of great significance. Then follow 'Bidayat al-Hidaya' on Sufism, 'al-Mustasfa' on usul al-fiqh, 'al-Jam' al-Awamm' (restraining the commoners from kalam), 'al-Radd 'ala al-Batiniyya' (refutation of the Batinis), 'Maqasid al-Falasifa' (objectives of the philosophers), 'Tahafut al-Falasifa' (incoherence of the philosophers), 'Jawahir al-Qur'an' (jewels of the Qur'an), 'Sharh al-Asma' al-Husna' (commentary on the beautiful names of Allah), 'Mishkat al-Anwar' (niche of lights), and 'al-Munqidh min al-Dalal' (deliverance from error). This comprehensive list shows Ghazali's breadth across jurisprudence, theology, philosophy, and spirituality.

Ghazali's Theological and Spiritual Works
List of Ghazali's major works in theology, philosophy, Sufism, and related fields.

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will understand the breadth of Ghazali's writings on theology, philosophy, Sufism, and Qur'anic spirituality.

Core Idea
Ghazali's greatest works span from the monumental Ihya to refutations of philosophers and Batinis.

While Ghazali is famed for his spiritual masterpiece Ihya' Ulum al-Din, he also produced critical works in philosophy and theology. This passage lists his key contributions in these areas, including books on usul al-fiqh, refutations, and spiritual guides.

Guided Path
1
Read the Arabic passage carefully
Read the Arabic text from 'وكتاب الإحياء' to the end. Note the variety of books mentioned, from Sufism to philosophy. Then read the translation.
The passage is a long list; try to group the books by topic.
2
Categorize the works
Identify which books are on theology (al-Jam' al-Awamm, al-Radd), philosophy (Maqasid, Tahafut), Sufism (Ihya, Bidayat al-Hidaya, Mishkat), and Qur'an (Jawahir, Sharh al-Asma').
Al-Mustasfa is on usul al-fiqh, but it also touches on theological methodology.
3
Understand the significance of 'al-Ihya'
The passage describes it as 'الأعجوبة العظيم الشأن' (the wonder of great importance). This highlights its central role in Ghazali's legacy.
Ihya' Ulum al-Din is a comprehensive work on Islamic spirituality.
4
Appreciate the diversity
Ask yourself why Ghazali wrote on so many different topics. This shows his concern for both intellectual and spiritual dimensions of Islam.

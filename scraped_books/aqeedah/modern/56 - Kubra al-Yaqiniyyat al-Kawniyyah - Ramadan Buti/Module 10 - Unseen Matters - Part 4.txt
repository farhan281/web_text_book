Refuting the Claim of Exemption for People of the Book
Author refutes the claim of exemption for People of the Book with Quranic evidence.

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will understand how the Quran explicitly includes the People of the Book in the universal threat of Hell, refuting any claim of exemption.

Core Idea
No one is exempt: the Quran groups all disbelievers under the same warning.

This lesson examines a crucial Quranic proof that demolishes the notion that the People of the Book will escape eternal punishment. The author presents a clear verse from Surah Al-Bayyinah and draws out its logical implication.

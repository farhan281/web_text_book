Learning Objective

Core Idea
Created causes have no real effect; all is directly from Allah.

One of the most distinctive doctrines of Ash'ari theology is its denial of causal efficacy in created things. In this passage, Sanusi asserts that the relationship between causes and their effects is merely habitual, not necessary. He provides a series of concrete examples to illustrate this principle, from food and satiety to fire and burning, and concludes that all originates from Allah without any intermediary.

Learning Objective

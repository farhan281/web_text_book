Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will understand why the phrase 'to the Day of Judgment' is interpreted as nearness of that day, and how 'the followers' refers to successive groups, not a specific group.

Core Idea
The Day of Judgment is near, not literal; followers are ongoing groups.

The correct understanding is that it refers to successive groups of followers, not a specific group. This repels the objection that the prayer would only include those who survive until the Day of Judgment, excluding earlier believers.

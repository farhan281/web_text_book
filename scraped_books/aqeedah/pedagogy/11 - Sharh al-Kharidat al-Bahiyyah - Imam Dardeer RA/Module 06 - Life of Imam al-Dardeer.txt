Commentary
This passage is a biography of Imam Ahmad al-Dardir, focusing on three aspects: his students, his works, and his personal qualities. The passage begins with a brief note on his education, mentioning his teachers Shams al-Din al-Hifni, al-Mallawi, and al-Jawhari. Then it lists prominent students who studied under him, including al-Dusuqi, al-Atbawi, al-Sawi, al-Sabai, and al-Fayyumi. Next, it enumerates his major works, numbering them from 1 to 11.

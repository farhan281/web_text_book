Introduction to the Aqaid Nasafiyyah Matn
The author describes the matn of Aqaid Nasafiyyah and its teaching context.

Learning Objective
By the end of this lesson, you will understand the background, content, and methodology of the Aqaid Nasafiyyah matn as introduced by the author.

Core Idea
The Aqaid Nasafiyyah is a concise creedal text following the methodology of Ahl al-Sunnah.

The author begins by introducing the matn that forms the foundation of this commentary. This passage tells us where this text was taught, what it covers, and whose methodology it follows. It sets the stage for the entire work.

Commentary
This passage describes the matn (text) of the book being commented upon. The author states that the matn is al-'Aqā'id al-Nasafiyyah, which they have placed within brackets. This matn was used as a textbook in the teaching institutes of al-Azhar and in several other countries. The matn presents the pillars of faith (īmān) and belief (i'tiqād) along with their subsidiary branches, but does so concisely. Importantly, it follows the methodology of Ahl al-Sunnah wa al-Jamā'ah, indicating its doctrinal affiliation. The phrase 'and their branches' (wa furū'ihi) indicates that the work covers not only the core creedal principles but also related secondary issues.

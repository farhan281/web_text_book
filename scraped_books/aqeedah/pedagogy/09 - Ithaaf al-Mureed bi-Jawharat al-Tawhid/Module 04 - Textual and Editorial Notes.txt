Core Idea
Small textual errors can alter key theological doctrines.

This passage is an editorial note from the editor of Jawharat al-Tawhid. He explains that he found several errors in the existing commentary that affect the meaning of important theological points. By examining the corrections, we learn why scholars of kalam pay meticulous attention to every word in classical texts.

This is why the editor took the trouble to produce a corrected edition.

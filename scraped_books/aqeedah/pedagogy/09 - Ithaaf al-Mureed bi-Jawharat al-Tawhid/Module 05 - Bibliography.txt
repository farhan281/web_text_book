Commentary
This passage catalogues the works of Abd al-Salam al-Laqqani. It lists four items: (1) 'Irshad al-Murid' – a short commentary on his father's poem 'Jawharat al-Tawhid.' (2) 'Ithaf al-Murid' – the present work, a longer commentary completed in 1047 AH, six years after his father's death. (3) A clarification about a supposed third commentary: al-Hamawi mentioned that al-Laqqani had three commentaries on the Jawhara, but the author explains that the third is actually a gloss ('hashiyah') on his father's intermediate commentary 'Hidayat al-Murid,' titled 'Fath al-Wasid li-Hidayat al-Murid.' Thus he has two commentaries and one gloss. (4) Two commentaries on the poem 'al-Jaza'iriyyah': one large and one concise, with copies of the concise one ('Fath al-Majid bi-Kifayat al-Murid') found in the Azhar library.
